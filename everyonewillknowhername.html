Everyone Will Know Her Name

By: Kira Koziak

The name Karma imprinted itself on her skin.
A woman written in the pages of beauty and delight.
Shut in the hardcover of a book you can't bear to finish,
her name was read in the billions.
Karma's synonyms lied in the length of her skirt,
how many drinks closed her mind,
and her continuous smile.
She hid in the street lights from grinning shadows
with daggers between her knuckles.
Ready to battle her way to survive a pleasant stroll home.

A man called Karma a bitch.
The bitch who bit back when he pulled her tail.
But her bark echoed in fury creating an army to take to war.
Caught in a whirlwind of social condemnation,
he called her a bitch when she refused
the touch that snaked its way to her flesh.
Having not realized her skin left a scar
branded on his reputation.

Karma's key for the revenge against those who wronged
relish in the words parading in the air.
Now awake, she demolished the chains holding her over the cliff.
Her wings spread with fury in her words.
Now nothing held her back.
When she set herself on fire to keep them warm,
the shadows thought she would burn in the malice.
But she flew with her feathers over the ones who scorned her.
Ready the demons and angels, Karma's war has just begun.
