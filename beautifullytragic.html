Beautifully Tragic

By: Kira Koziak

As a girl, I always knew the world to be loud
A place of never-ending sound
But,
I find myself in silence.

Upon a story's curse,
You find your way back
To the path you drew
The place you created with your old soul.

But memory is only a tale
I keep telling myself
To live in the fiction
Of my own mind.

Yet nothing warned me
Of the upcoming storm
Soon to take place
On the canvass of the battlefield

But has any moon thought of the desire
In destruction of stars?
For how else do I explain
The beauty I found in chaos.

You are my first darkness
My first monster
And you are the reason
For why I don't turn out the lights.
